    Heart Rate

    Your heart rate is the number of times your heart beats per minute.

    Blood Oxygen

    When you inhale oxygen, it goes to your bloodstream through your lungs. This amount of oxygen present in your blood is called blood oxygen level.

    Sleep

    An average adult should get atleast 7-9 hours of sleep.

    Stress

    Stress, in the context of health, refers to the body's response to physical, emotional, or psychological challenges or demands.

    Steps

    Steps refer to the number of individual movements taken by a person while walking or running. An average person should take atleast 10000 steps a day.

    Distance

    Distance refers to the extent or amount of space between two points, typically measured in units such as meters, kilometers, miles, or feet.

    Blood Glucose Level

    Blood glucose level refers to the concentration of glucose (sugar) present in the bloodstream at any given time.

    Oxygen Saturation

    Oxygen saturation, often abbreviated as SpO2, is a measure of the percentage of hemoglobin in the blood that is saturated with oxygen.

    Breath Rate

    Breathing rate, also known as respiratory rate or ventilation rate, refers to the number of breaths taken per minute.
